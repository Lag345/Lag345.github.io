To identify the prime members

The prime members are: Antareep Roy, Aditya Chakraborty and Whibor Roy.

To set up new rules and regulations and to strictly abide by them

Part X: The organisation

  1. Transcendence, our organisation will have the only ambition- to not be confined in boundary.

Part B:  Membership

  1. At the commencement of this rule, every person (a) who was the founding members of the SDG  or (b) either has cooperated with the founding members for 5 years shall be the prime members (c) has been approved by all the prime members.
  2. Even though a person is eligible to be a prime member through Part B article 2, he/she can be demoted to domestic members by the identified prime members during the commencement of these rules but cannot be rejected to give a membership.

Part C:  Powers of the members

  1. The domestic members have virtually no powers. They have to communicate with prime members to bring out a proposal.
  2. No decisions can be carried out without consulting the prime minister. Each member has a veto power i.e a member can singly reject a proposal.
  3. If the bill is necessary the proposal can be carried only if it is supported by majority. The Prime member who has rejected the bill may or may not act according to the bill.

Conclusion

  1. Modifying the rules or any part of the organisation

The rules or any part of the organisation can be modified if only all prime members agree.
